About Me

I'm a Data Scientist with a background in healthcare analytics, generative AI, and large-scale data systems. At CVS Health, I've worked on high-impact projects that blend engineering, analytics, and business strategy, driving millions in cost savings and transforming how teams operate.

A major focus of my work has been applying GenAI and large language models to understand complex healthcare documents and generate production ready code, reducing our reliance on domain experts and accelerating development cycles.

One of my most rewarding experiences has been leading the end-to-end development of a 0-1 pipeline that enabled a new business unit. This initiative involved direct coordination with senior leadership across technical and non-technical teams and earned internal recognition through an Premier Award.

I hold a Master's in Data Science from Columbia University and a Bachelor's in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering from IIT Madras. I'm passionate about using GenAI and applied analytics to solve high-stakes, real-world problems.
